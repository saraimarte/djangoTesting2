About

My father's job is to deliver newspapers to various stores. On May 2023, his boss added many new stops to his route but didn't provide him with an updated route.
 
Shortly after that, I found my father manually going through each of his stops and trying to find out where to place the new stops in his route. He spent the whole day working on it.
 
I felt bad because I knew that I could help him. So I did.
 
I went and automated his actions into a nice Python Script.
 
At that time, despite knowing Python, HTML, CSS, and a bit of Vanilla JS, I had no idea how to use my Python code in a frontend site so it stayed as a script.
 
Fast forward September 2023 I had learned the basics of django and managed to create the frontend all the while using the Python Script in the backend leading to the creation of...RouteSync.
